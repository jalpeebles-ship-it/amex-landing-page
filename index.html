Questions

Is this really free?

Yes. The tracker and monthly email reports are completely free. We plan to add premium features for multi-card tracking in the future, but the Amex Platinum coach will remain free.

Do I need to link my bank account?

No. We never ask for your card number, login credentials, or any financial data. You just tell us which benefits you've enrolled in and log your usage manually.

On the 1st of every month, we send you a digest showing which credits you've used, what's about to expire, anything you haven't enrolled in, and whether your card is paying for itself.
